# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
MasjidOS is a modern, multi-tenant SaaS platform designed to streamline mosque management. It provides a centralized dashboard for financial recording, donation management, activity scheduling, inventory tracking, and document organization, aiming to increase transparency and operational efficiency for religious institutions.

## Target Users
- **Mosque Administrators (DKM):** For overall management and oversight of mosque operations.
- **Treasurers (Bendahara):** To manage finances, record income/expenses, and generate reports.
- **Secretaries:** For managing documents, announcements, and administrative tasks.
- **Volunteers & Staff:** To coordinate activities and manage inventory.
- **Congregants (Jamaah) & Donors:** To access information, events, and make donations transparently.

## User Goals
- **Transparency:** Ensure all financial transactions and mosque activities are transparent and audit-ready.
- **Efficiency:** Reduce manual paperwork and streamline administrative workflows.
- **Accessibility:** Provide easy access to mosque information for administrators and congregants alike.
- **Trust:** Build trust within the community through clear reporting and communication.

## Key Features
- **Dashboard Overview:** Real-time KPI cards, recent transactions, and upcoming events.
- **Financial Management:** comprehensive recording of income and expenses with category filtering.
- **Donation Management:** Tools for tracking donors, handling anonymous contributions, and public donation links.
- **Activity Scheduling:** Event calendars and volunteer management.
- **Inventory Tracking:** Asset management including condition and value tracking.
- **Document Storage:** Centralized repository for proposals, reports, and other documents.
- **Announcements:** Broadcast system for internal and public news.
- **Role-Based Access Control:** Secure access management for different user roles.
- **Audit Logs:** Full history of system changes for accountability.
